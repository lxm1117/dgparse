===== Run 3 ======

Found 207 records in the cds table that are linked to the wrong gene i.e. 
cds points to one gene
cds points to transcript via name/ccds_accession and transcript points to a different gene

Due to
a) gene name mapping in dgsource/manifest/manifest/adapters/ncbi/ccds_loader.py
b) gene names are not unique


Example:

select g.accession, g.name, g.id, ch.name, c.start_end 
from gene g, coordinates c, chromosome ch where 
g.track_id = c.id and 
c.chromosome_id = ch.id and g.name = 'STMN3';

cds.id, cds_gene.accession, cds_gene.name, cds_gene.biotype,
transcript.ccds_accession, cds_transcript_gene.accession, cds_transcript_gene.name, cds_transcript_gene.biotype
from 
gene cds_gene, gene cds_transcript_gene, cds, transcript 
where
cds.gene_id = cds_gene.id and
cds.name = transcript.ccds_accession and
transcript.gene_id = cds_transcript_gene.id
